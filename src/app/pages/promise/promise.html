My Promise

From the moment I sat beside you that morning, around 10:30 on January 1st, I didn’t just see the most beautiful eyes in the world — I saw my future. In that simple auto ride, with no fireworks and no grand celebration, something inside me became very clear. I don’t just want moments with you. I don’t just want memories. I want a life with you. I want ordinary mornings, random conversations, quiet car rides, and soft smiles that steal my breath away. I want to be the person you turn to when you’re happy, when you’re tired, when you’re proud, and when you need comfort. I want to hold your hand not just for a moment, but for years. I want to grow with you, build with you, laugh with you, and face everything life brings — together. So today, with all the honesty in my heart, I’m asking you… will you let me choose you forever? Will you let me be the man who stands beside you not just in love, but in life? Will you marry me? ❤️
