Your Smile 😄✨💖

And then you smiled. It wasn’t a big dramatic smile. It was simple. Natural. Effortless. But the moment your lips curved and your eyes lit up, something inside me stopped. For a second, I forgot how to breathe. I forgot what I was thinking. I forgot everything except you. Your smile has this power over me. It doesn’t just make you look beautiful — it changes the entire atmosphere around you. That auto ride, that ordinary road, that simple morning… suddenly felt warmer. Brighter. Softer. When you smile, it feels like the world becomes quiet just so I can admire you properly. All I wanted in that moment was to pull you closer and hug you — not because I was cold, not because I needed comfort, but because my heart felt so full that I didn’t know what else to do with all that emotion.
