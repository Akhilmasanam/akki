Your Eyes

I can’t be completely sure, because I was so lost in you that time didn’t even matter. When our eyes met, it felt like the world paused for a second. In the middle of the moving traffic and ordinary morning noise, your eyes looked so calm, so soft, so unbelievably beautiful that I forgot about everything else. I didn’t need fireworks or loud celebrations to feel something magical. That quiet morning, in that simple auto ride, looking into your eyes — that was my celebration. And if my year began at 10:30 AM with me staring into the most beautiful eyes in the world, then I already know this year is going to be special… because it started with you. ❤️✨
